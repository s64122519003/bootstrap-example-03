Jaguar

The jaguar is a large cat species and the only living member of the genus Panthera native to the Americas. With a body length of up to 1.85 m (6 ft 1 in) and a weight of up to 158 kg (348 lb), Its distinctively marked coat features pale yellow to tan colored fur covered by spots that transition to rosettes on the sides.

African bush elephant

African bush elephants are distributed across 37 African countries and inhabits forests, grasslands and woodlands, wetlands and agricultural land.



Asian elephant

Asian elephants frequently inhabit grasslands, tropical evergreen forests, semi-evergreen forests, moist deciduous forests, dry deciduous forests and dry thorn forests.
